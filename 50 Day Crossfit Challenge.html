General Notes:
  • "DB" = Dumbbell.
  • Adjust weights based on your strength for the target rep range. The last few reps should be challenging but doable with good form.
  • "AMRAP" = As Many Rounds/Reps As Possible in the given time.
  • "RFT" = Rounds For Time. Complete the work as fast as possible with good form.
  • "EMOM" = Every Minute On the Minute. Start the prescribed work at the beginning of each minute. Rest for the remainder of the minute.
  • "For Quality" = Focus on controlled movement and perfect form, not speed.
  • **Rest:** While 50 workout days are provided, integrating 1-2 rest days per week is crucial for recovery and long-term progress. Listen to your body!
